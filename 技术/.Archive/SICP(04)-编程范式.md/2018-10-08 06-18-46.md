## 1.1 局部状态值
当设计一个银行账户系统的时候，很自然的用一个变量`balance`保存账户余额，每次取款`withdraw`和存款`deposit`都更新账户余额，初步系统设计如下：
```scheme
(define  (make-account balance)
    (define (withdraw amount)
        (if (>= balance amount)
                (begin (set! balance (- balance amount))
                    balance)
                    "Insufficie"
```
